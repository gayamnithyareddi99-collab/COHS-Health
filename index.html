About COHS Health

COHS Health (CareHome Oral Health Sentinel) is a UK-based health-tech platform designed to transform how oral health is managed inside care homes by turning clinical guidance into a structured, operational care pathway.

The platform addresses a critical gap where oral health is expected to be delivered consistently but often remains informal, inconsistently recorded, and difficult to monitor across daily care routines.

COHS Health creates a continuous workflow from admission assessment to daily care, ongoing monitoring, and structured escalation with clinician-ready evidence helping care homes detect issues earlier, improve care consistency, and maintain audit-ready governance at all times.

How COHS Health Works

COHS Health transforms oral health in care homes into a structured, continuous care pathway from admission baseline to daily care, ongoing surveillance, and clinician-ready escalation ensuring early detection, consistent delivery, and audit-ready governance.

01

Admission Baseline Assessment

At admission, care staff complete a structured oral-health baseline capturing dentition, dentures, symptoms, dryness, and support needs. This ensures every resident starts with a clear, standardised foundation instead of inconsistent documentation.

02

Daily Care Task Execution

COHS converts assessment findings into simple daily care actions such as brushing support, denture cleaning, hydration, and comfort checks making oral care consistent, visible, and operational in everyday workflows.

03

Ongoing Surveillance & Monitoring

The platform supports scheduled and event-triggered monitoring with structured observations and image capture enabling early detection of deterioration instead of waiting for visible or painful symptoms.

04

Risk Detection & Escalation

COHS identifies deterioration patterns and classifies residents into Routine, Monitor, or Escalate states generating clinician-ready escalation bundles with evidence, history, and observations for faster and better decision-making.

What is COHS Health?

COHS (CareHome Oral Health Sentinel) is a UK health-tech platform that transforms oral health into a structured care pathway including assessment, daily care, monitoring, and escalation.
